What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, measure visitor behavior, and improve your browsing experience.

Cookies We Use

1. Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These are required for the site to function and cannot be disabled.

Cookie Purpose Duration

2. Analytics Cookies (Require Consent)

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. No personally identifiable information is transmitted.
