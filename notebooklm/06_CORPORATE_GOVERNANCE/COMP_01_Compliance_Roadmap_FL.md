# COMPLIANCE & TAX ROADMAP: FLORIDA & HILLSBOROUGH COUNTY

This roadmap outlines the mandatory bureaucratic filings and tax compliance requirements for Nivo Partners LLC operating as a professional services firm.

## 1. STATE-LEVEL COMPLIANCE (SUNBIZ)

### Annual Report Filing

- **Entity**: Florida Department of State (Division of Corporations).
- **Deadline**: Between January 1st and May 1st each year.
- **Action**: File via [Sunbiz.org](https://sunbiz.org).
- **Required Info**: Officer/Manager names, registered agent, and principal address.
- **Note**: Failure to file by May 1st results in a mandatory $400 late fee and potential administrative dissolution.

## 2. COUNTY-LEVEL COMPLIANCE (HILLSBOROUGH)

### Business Tax Receipt (BTR)

- **Entity**: Hillsborough County Tax Collector.
- **Action**: Obtain the Local Business Tax Receipt (formerly Occupational License).
- **Application**: Can be done online via the [Hillsborough County Tax Collector website](https://www.hillstax.org).
- **Requirement**: Professional service providers must show proof of relevant state licensing if applicable.
- **Renewal**: Annual renewal is required by September 30th.

### Tangible Personal Property (TPP) Tax

- **Requirement**: Any business that owns equipment (computers, servers, furniture) used in the business must file a Return of Tangible Personal Property (Form DR-405) with the County Property Appraiser by April 1st.
- **Exemption**: There is an initial $25,000 exemption if you file the return on time.

- **Status**: Professional services (Computer Systems Design) are generally **NOT taxable** in Florida.
