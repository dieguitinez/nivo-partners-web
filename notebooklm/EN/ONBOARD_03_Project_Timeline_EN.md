## ðŸ“‹ Milestones That Require Client Action

These are points where **the client must approve or deliver** before work can proceed. Missing these creates cascading delays.

| Milestone | Client action required | Maximum wait before escalation |
|---|---|---|
| Asset submission | Photos, copy, logins, brand guide | 5 business days after kickoff |
| Wireframe approval | Written sign-off (email is sufficient) | 3 business days |
| Revision round | Consolidated feedback (one email, all changes) | 3 business days |
| Domain/DNS approval | Technical access or DNS record confirmation | 2 business days |
| Launch approval | Final "go ahead" from client | 2 business days |

**Escalation script when client stalls:**
> *"We're at [milestone] and need your [asset/approval] to move forward. Every day we wait pushes the delivery date by one day. If we don't hear back by [date + 3 days], we'll schedule a 15-min check-in call to review."*
