### Why an LLC?

- **Personal liability protection:** Client disputes, contractor issues, or errors cannot touch personal assets

## ğŸ“„ Contracts

### When a Contract is Required

- **Always** before receiving any payment from a client
- Before starting any work, without exception
- Before granting access to client systems or credentials

### Minimum Contract Elements

Every Nivo Partners client contract must include:

| Clause | Description |
|---|---|
| Scope of work | Exact deliverables, platforms, and what is NOT included |
| Timeline | Projected milestones and final delivery date |
| Payment terms | Amounts, due dates, non-refundable deposit clause |
| Revision policy | Number of revision rounds included |
| IP ownership transfer | When ownership transfers (after final payment) |
| Client responsibilities | What the client must provide and by when |
| Termination clause | How either party can exit the contract |
| Governing law | State of Florida |

### Signature Protocol

- Digital signature via DocuSign or HelloSign (legally binding)
- Both parties must sign before work begins
- Signed contract stored in client folder in Drive

## ğŸ”’ Intellectual Property

### Work Product Ownership

- All deliverables created for a client are owned by **Nivo Partners** until final payment is received
- Upon receipt of final payment, **IP transfers fully to the client**
- This applies to: website code, design files, ad campaigns, AI agent configurations, documentation

### Proprietary Components

The following are **NOT transferred** to clients regardless of payment:

- Nivo Partners' internal methodologies and frameworks (The Core, The Shield, The Effectors)
- Kai AI architecture and base conversational framework
- Nivo Partners' brand assets, visual identity, and marketing materials
- Internal documentation and pricing guides

### Client Data

- Client credentials and access tokens are stored securely (Bitwarden) and never shared externally
- Upon contract termination, client receives full access handover within 5 business days
- Nivo Partners retains no access to client systems after formal termination
