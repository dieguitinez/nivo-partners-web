## üí° Pricing Policy

### When to apply discounts

- **Full upfront payment (100%):** 5% off setup
- **Signed 6-month retainer:** 10% off setup
- **Active referral (client referred someone):** $300 credit on next invoice

### When NOT to lower the price

- If the client says "it's expensive" before understanding the value ‚Üí present ROI (DECK_05)
- If the client asks for more than the package includes ‚Üí add as add-on, don't concede
- If comparing to $500 freelancers ‚Üí *"That service and this one are not the same product"*

### Payment structure

| Moment | % of total |
|---|---|
| On contract signing | 50% (non-refundable deposit) |
| On wireframe/structure approval | 25% |
| On final delivery | 25% |

> For projects >$10,000, can be structured as 3 payments of 33.3% each.

### Monthly retainer

- Billed on the 1st of each month
- Does not include out-of-scope work ‚Äî add-ons are quoted separately
- Cancellation: 30 days written notice
